Reach

REACH, Regulation No 1907/2006 on the Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restriction of Chemicals.

REACH requires that sufficient information on Substances of Very High Concern (SVHC) contained in articles in concentration above 0.1% weight by weight (w/w) is communicated to allow the safe use of the product.

The following tables cover 191 REACH SVHCs which are updated by ECHA on 27th June, 2018. (http://echa.europa.eu/candidate-list-table)

The following products contain a SVHC in a concentration above 0.1% w/w.

None.
